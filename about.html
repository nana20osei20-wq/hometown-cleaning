Our Story

Built on trust,
powered by people.

Hometown Co started in 2009 when founder Abena Mensah wanted to give families in Accra access to reliable, professional cleaning they could genuinely trust. What began as a small team of three has grown into a company of over 30 dedicated professionals.

We believe a clean environment changes how you feel in your home. That's why we train every team member not just in technique, but in care — treating every property as if it were our own.

What We Stand For

Our core values

Everything we do stems from six principles that have guided us since day one.

Reliability

We show up on time, every time. Our clients count on us, and we take that responsibility seriously — no excuses, no surprises.

Genuine Care

We treat every home like our own. Our team is trained to notice the details that matter most, and to handle your belongings with complete respect.

Trust & Safety

Every team member is background-checked, insured, and trained. You can leave us with your keys and your peace of mind stays intact.

Excellence

Good enough is never good enough for us. We hold ourselves to a high standard in every corner of every room, every single visit.

Eco-Conscious

We use biodegradable, non-toxic products that are safe for children, pets, and the planet. Clean homes, clean conscience.

Community

We hire locally, invest in our team, and give back to the neighbourhood we serve. When Accra thrives, we thrive together.
